We haven't enough to ship. I am going to learn engraving with a German engraver there, and then try to get work in Chicago.”

Alexandra's hands dropped in her lap. Her eyes became dreamy and filled with tears.

Carl's sensitive lower lip trembled. He scratched in the soft earth beside him with a stick. “That's all I hate about it, Alexandra,” he said slowly. “You've stood by us through so much and helped father out so many times, and now it seems as if we were running off and leaving you to face the worst of it.
